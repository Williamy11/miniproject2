Embark on a 10-day journey through Thailand's most iconic and picturesque destinations. Explore the bustling streets of Bangkok, visit historic temples, and shop for souvenirs at the famous Chatuchak Weekend Market. Then head to the charming city of Chiang Mai, nestled in the mountains, to experience the laid-back vibe and rich culture. Take a scenic drive through the mountains to the bohemian town of Pai, surrounded by lush greenery and stunning waterfalls. Finally, relax on the paradise island of Phuket, stay in a luxurious beachfront resort and soak up the sun on some of Thailand's most beautiful beaches. Our travel package includes all flights, accommodation, ground transportation, and some meals, so you can focus on enjoying this unforgettable journey through Thailand.

tour

Private tour guide: One feature that could add extra value to your travel package would be a private tour guide who will accompany you throughout your journey. This guide could provide insider knowledge of each destination, share local stories and history, and give you personalized recommendations on where to eat, shop, and explore.

restaurant

Street food tour: Thailand is known for its delicious and varied street food, and a street food tour could be a great addition to your travel package. You could sample local delicacies, learn about the ingredients and preparation techniques, and experience the bustling atmosphere of street food markets in each destination.

scuba_diving

Scuba diving excursion: Phuket is a popular destination for scuba diving, and an excursion could be a thrilling addition to your itinerary. You could explore coral reefs, see colorful tropical fish and other marine life, and experience the underwater world of the Andaman Sea. This could be a great opportunity for both experienced divers and beginners who want to try something new.
